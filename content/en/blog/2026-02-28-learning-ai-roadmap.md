A log of actionable steps to understand AI from first principles. You can follow this path to repeat the journey.

## Steps

1. **[First Principles Thinking](/blog/2025/09/02/first-principles-thinking/)** — Before diving into AI, develop the mental framework for reasoning from fundamentals. This is the foundation everything else builds on.

2. **On Intelligence** by Jeff Hawkins — A theory of how the neocortex works and what intelligence actually is. Essential reading for understanding what we're trying to build.
